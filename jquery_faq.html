Pearl Jam FAQ

How long has Pearl Jam been together?
Pearl Jam was established in 1990.
Who is the singer of Pearl Jam?
Eddie Vedder is the frontman since 1990.
Does the singer write his own songs?
Eddie is Pearl Jam's singer, songwriter, guitarist and ukuleleist.
Who is the lead guitarist for Pearl Jam?
Mike McCready is lead guitarist and a founding member.
Who is the bass guitarist for Pearl Jam?
Jeff Ament is the bass guitarist and is a founding member.
Who is the rhythm guitarist for Pearl Jam?
Stone Gossard is the rhythm guitarist and a founding member.
Who is the drummer for the band?
Matt Cameron is the drummer for Pearl Jam and Soundgarden.
When and what was the band's first album?
Pearl Jam's debut album is Ten and was released on August 27, 1991.
Do the band member's have any solo projects?
Eddie Vedder also has several solo albums, as well as do other member's of the band.
Is Pearl Jam a candidate for the Rock Hall of Fame?
Pearl Jam was inducted into the Rock Hall of Fame in 2017.
